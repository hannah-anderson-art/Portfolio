About Me

Hello! I'm a UI/UX designer and aspiring developer from Austin, TX. Currently pursuing a BS in Visualization at Texas A&M University, I'll be graduating in May 2024. My coursework has spanned traditional art, design, and various programming for visualization courses.

In 2022, I interned as a graphic designer at the George Bush Presidential Library and Museum, working on ads, promotions, and the annual Easter egg logo. Over the past two years at Texas A&M's Engineering Studio for Advanced Instruction and Learning (eSAIL), I've been deeply involved in a grant project, contributing to Python courses for the Texas public. As the student coordinator, I worked on a 'capstone project' designing the template for a Growth and Promotion (GAP) course and creating an instructional video.

I'm excited about the intersection of design and technology, and I look forward to bringing my creativity and skills to innovative projects.
